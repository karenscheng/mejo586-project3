City of Seattle Wages: Comparison by Gender


The statistic of women earning $0.78 for every dollar that men earn is true when it comes to comparing net, annual salary for women and men in the US. However, there are many factors that contribute to this gap, and is not so simple as a matter of women making less money for doing the exact same job as men, as Equal Pay Day will lead you to believe. In this dataset, we are comparing the salaries of women and men in Seattle, and controlling for differences in job title. We will explore other factors that may possibly contribute to the gender wage gap.
